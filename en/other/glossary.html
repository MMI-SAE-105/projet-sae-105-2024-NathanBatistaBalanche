Glossary

Here is the definition of the most important words to understand

Board Games

Central term of the text, referring to a variety of games involving multiple participants, often around a board or cards, and aiming to create social interactions.

Strategy

Key element in many board games, referring to planning and long-term decisions to maximize chances of victory.

Augmented Reality (AR)

Technology that combines virtual elements with the real world, potentially integrated into future board games to create immersive experiences.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Mentioned as a future tool in board games to adjust scenarios or adapt the difficulty level for players.

Cooperative Games

Category of games where players work together to achieve a common goal, in contrast to competitive games.

Role-Playing Games (RPG)

Genre of games where participants embody characters, evolving in complex narrative scenarios.

Immersion

Concept related to the gaming experience, particularly with AR and VR technologies, where players are immersed in an interactive and visually captivating world.

Virtual Reality (VR)

Immersive technology mentioned in the context of future board games, where players interact in an entirely virtual world.

Modularity

Mentioned in the text in reference to future games, referring to the ability to customize and adjust a game to vary the gaming experience.

Kickstarter

Crowdfunding platform, cited as a way to enable the creation of new games.

Bluff

Technique mentioned in the analysis of "Poker Face", referring to the concealment of intentions in strategy games.

Cognitive Skill

Refers to the mental and intellectual abilities that board games can help develop, such as logic and problem-solving.

Territorial Skill

Game mechanic particularly referenced in games like Risk, where players must expand their dominance over a map or territory.

Mass Production

Term used in the text to refer to the large-scale production of board games during industrialization, which allowed their popularization.

Eco-Responsibility

Concept discussed regarding the future of board games, which may integrate sustainable materials and environmentally friendly practices.

Interactive Boards

Technological innovation mentioned in the future of board games, where boards would be able to interact with players or adapt the game in real-time.
